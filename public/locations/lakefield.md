# Mercury Repower for Lakefield, Ontario Boaters
Harris Boat Works is 45 minutes south of Lakefield in Gores Landing, ON, on the south shore of Rice Lake. The drive is roughly 45 km through Peterborough. We're a Mercury Platinum Dealer, selling Mercury since 1965 and family-owned since 1947. Lakefield sits in the northern Kawarthas near Stony Lake, big-water territory with bigger boats and heavier repowers. We rig, install, and water-test on Rice Lake before pickup at Gores Landing. No shipping. Typical full repower: $11,000–$40,000 CAD, often at the higher end for Stony Lake hulls.

## FAQs
### How far is Harris Boat Works from Lakefield?

About 45 minutes south, roughly 45 km via Peterborough on Highway 28 and out County Road 9 into Gores Landing on the south shore of Rice Lake.

### Do you deliver Mercury motors to Lakefield or Stony Lake cottages?

No. We're pickup-only at Gores Landing. We rig and water-test on Rice Lake, then you drive south to pick up.

### What HP Mercury is common for Stony Lake and northern Kawartha boats?

Stony Lake boats often run bigger. Pro XS V8 4.6L 200–300 HP and FourStroke big-block 150–250 HP are common repowers in this segment, sometimes Verado V8/V10 250–400 HP on special order.

### Can I drop my boat at HBW and pick it up later?

Yes. Many Lakefield customers drop the boat with us during the quoting visit and pick it up after the rigging and water test. Typical turnaround is 1–3 weeks once the motor lands.

### Can Lakefield customers finance a Mercury repower?

Yes. Financing is 7.99% APR over $10,000 and 8.99% APR under $10,000, OAC. Deposits are $200 portable, $500 mid-range, $1,000 big-block, Pro XS, or special-order Verado.

## Service boundary
Harris Boat Works does not perform mobile service, on-site installs, or driveway/marina visits in Lakefield. Customers from Lakefield bring their boat to our Gores Landing shop, or pick up a loose Mercury motor for self-install.
