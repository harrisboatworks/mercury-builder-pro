No. Pickup only at Gores Landing with photo ID. We do not ship motors and we do not deliver, strict industry-wide fraud-prevention policy.

### Do you do mobile service in the GTA?

No. Harris Boat Works is a shop-based Mercury dealer in Gores Landing. We do not perform on-site installs, driveway service, or marina visits in any GTA city.

For real CAD pricing online, Mercury-only expertise since 1965, Premier-tier customer-satisfaction status, and a clean pickup walk-through that protects your warranty registration.
