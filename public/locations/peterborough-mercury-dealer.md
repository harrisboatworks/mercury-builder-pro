# Mercury Service and Repower Near Peterborough

Harris Boat Works is a Mercury Premier Dealer and Mercury Certified Repower Centre in Gores Landing, about 35 minutes south of Peterborough. Customers bring their own boats for scheduled Mercury and MerCruiser work. HBW does not provide or arrange transport.

## FAQs

### How far is Harris Boat Works from Peterborough?

About 35 minutes in normal traffic. The usual route runs south on Highway 28 and east on County Road 9 toward Gores Landing. Check live directions before towing.

### Can Peterborough customers get a Mercury quote before driving?

Yes. The pricing reference shows current bare-motor selling prices in CAD. The quote builder provides an installed starting point. HBW confirms the final price and written scope.

### Does HBW deliver to Peterborough?

No. Customers bring their boats to Gores Landing and return for pickup. HBW does not provide or arrange transport.

### Does HBW water-test completed repowers?

Yes. Completed repowers are water-tested on Rice Lake before customer pickup.
