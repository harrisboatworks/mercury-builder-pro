## FAQs

### Do I really need a kicker for musky fishing?

Not required, but highly recommended for serious trolling. A kicker gives precise low speeds, saves main motor hours, and is more fuel efficient during extended trolling sessions.

### What about running rivers for musky?

Rivers require adequate power to handle current. We recommend at least 115HP for the Trent system. More power provides safety margin when water is high.

### Tiller or remote for musky fishing?

Most serious musky anglers prefer remote. Boats are larger, and helm position gives better visibility for spotting fish. Kicker can be tiller for trolling control.

### How important is Command Thrust for musky?

Less critical than for walleye. Musky areas typically have adequate depth. Standard lower unit works well. That said, Command Thrust doesn't hurt.
