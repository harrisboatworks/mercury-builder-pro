## FAQs

### Is SeaPro more expensive than FourStroke?

Slightly, but the extended service intervals and commercial-grade components provide value for high-hour users. For recreational use under 200 hours/year, FourStroke is usually the better value.

### Can I use SeaPro recreationally?

Absolutely. Many recreational boaters choose SeaPro for the extended service intervals and durability. It's not just for commercial use—it's for anyone who wants commercial-grade reliability.

### What's the difference in warranty?

SeaPro can qualify for Mercury's commercial warranty program, which has different terms than recreational warranty. We can explain the specifics for your situation.

### How many hours can a SeaPro handle?

With proper maintenance, SeaPro motors routinely run 3,000-5,000+ hours. We have customers with over 5,000 hours on SeaPro motors still going strong.
