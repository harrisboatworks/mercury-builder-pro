## The structure: 3 base + 4 promotional bonus = 7 total

Mercury's contractual Canadian warranty is 3 years limited + 3 years corrosion running concurrently (not stacked, not additive). That is the floor. It applies on every new Mercury sold by any authorized dealer in Canada, with or without a promo.

On top of that, Harris Boat Works is running a 4-year promotional bonus on every new Mercury outboard we sell through June 14, 2026. The total: 7 years of factory-backed coverage.

- The coverage is factory-backed Mercury warranty work, handled by Mercury and its authorized dealer network. It is not a third-party service contract.
- The 4 bonus years are HBW's promotion. Mercury's own consumer "Get 7" program has ended. This is our offer, on top of the 3-year factory base.
- Coverage is non-declining. A covered failure in year 6 is treated the same as month 1: parts and labour, no deductible.
- After June 14, 2026, the bonus years revert. New Mercury outboards purchased after that date fall back to the 3-year standard, unless a new promotion is announced.

## What the 7-year coverage actually covers

- The powerhead: block, head, internal components, fuel injection
- The lower unit: gearcase, shaft, prop bearings
- The mid-section: driveshaft, exhaust housing
- Electronics: ECU, charging system, ignition, sensors
- Cooling system: water pump housing, thermostat, sensors
- Starting and charging: starter motor, alternator, regulator

Parts and labour are covered at any authorized Mercury dealer in Canada when a claim is approved.

## What is not covered

## Registration, claims, and transfer

We register every Mercury at delivery, on the spot. For a claim: call (905) 342-2153, bring the boat and motor in for diagnosis, and we submit the claim directly to Mercury. ## How it relates to MPP and TD financing

Mercury Product Protection (MPP) is Mercury's separate paid extended service program, quoted at time of sale. It picks up after factory coverage ends and is administered by Mercury Marine. The 7-year HBW Exclusive promo is included in the purchase price of every new Mercury we sell through June 14, 2026; MPP is optional and quoted separately.

The 7-year warranty promotion and TD financing at 5.48% are separate program categories and apply together on qualifying Mercury outboards purchased through HBW during the promotional window. Financing is OAC.

## After June 14, 2026

When the promotion ends, the bonus years revert. New Mercury outboards purchased after that date fall back to the 3-year standard contractual base, unless a new promotion is announced. Purchases registered by June 14, 2026 lock in the full 7 years.

### Is the 7 years from Mercury or from HBW?

### What does the warranty cover?
